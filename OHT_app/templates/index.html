Welcome to the BEA Ontario Health Team

About Us

The BRAMPTON/ETOBICOKE AND AREA ONTARIO HEALTH TEAM is a collaboration of health care organizations who have started to work together to improve the coordination of care among family physicians, hospitals, long term care facilities, and community care

The group came together to submit an application to become an Ontario Health Team. The official announcement of teams by the Ministry of Health and Long-Term Care will take place in the fall of 2019.

Our Team

Current members of the team include

  • Region of Peel
  • William Osler Health System
  • Family Health Teams
  • And So Many More!

Our Approach To Care

The BEA Ontario Health Team is committed to creating a model of care to ensure there is a connected system of care for all health care providers and agencies in North Toronto. We will work together to ensure patients are accessing the right care when they need it
